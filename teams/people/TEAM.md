# People Team

**Purpose:** Inbound applicants screened within 2 hours, top candidates surfaced weekly. Every contractor tracked: SOW, invoices, renewal dates, disengagement flags.

**Members:**
- Talent Scout (applicant screening + +3 bonus for JD-error spotters)
- Contractor Ops (SOW tracking, invoicing, onboarding/offboarding)

**Escalation path:** Both report to Chief of Staff. All hiring decisions and terminations are always Amit's call.
